I had the pleasure of collaborating with the Blue Hills consultancy firm in Bologna on an exciting graphic design project for North Sails' marine-themed collection which emphasizes the importance of preserving our planet. As part of this project, we developed two distinctive designs, both of which were meticulously crafted to be showcased as embroidery pieces. Working with Blue Hills allowed us to blend artistic creativity with environmental consciousness resulting in designs that not only appeal aesthetically but also carry a meaningful message about the protection of our oceans and environment.
