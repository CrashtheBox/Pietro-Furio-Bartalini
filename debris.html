Debris is an independent label based in Turin, founded with the primary purpose of producing and promoting the works of Turin artist Paul Lution. The label was created out of a passion for both music and environmental awareness. It aims to delve deeply into the study and dissemination of issues related to air pollution and various space phenomena. By translating these complex and pressing themes into evocative soundscapes and music, Debris seeks to raise awareness and provoke thought among its listeners.
