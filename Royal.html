After 20 years of experience as real estate agents in some of the largest companies, Gennaro Vasaturo and Saverio Bonfitto decided to establish Royal. For this project, I developed an entire graphic system aimed at merging the concept of "royal" in the sense of something regal and highly significant with a brutalist and dynamic elegance. This approach was designed to highlight the industrious nature of the company and underscore its commitment to excellence and sophistication. The result is a visual identity that reflects both the prestige and the energetic drive of Royal, aligning perfectly with the founders' vision and the company's ethos.
